Major Achivements

  • Founding the Maratha Empire

  • He was a master of guerrilla warfare, using innovative tactics and the terrain to his advantage against larger and better-equipped armies.

  • Building a Strong Navy

  • Despite being a devout Hindu, Shivaji Maharaj welcomed Muslims into his service and protected places of worship of all faiths.

  • He bravely resisted the Mughal emperor Aurangzeb and his forces, earning the title of "Chhatrapati," which signifies a supreme ruler.

  • Shivaji's vision of an independent Indian nation, known as "Hindavi Swarajya," continues to inspire generations of Indians.
